Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: April 3, 2025

Richard Koranteng LLC ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. When you schedule an appointment or register for our service(s), we may collect:

  • Appointment and Registration Data: Information submitted through Google Forms and Google Calendar for scheduling.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, as we use third-party services like Google Forms and Google Calendar, their privacy policies also apply. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.
