Answers that help you move forward with confidence

Do you offer training?

Yes, we offer training programs that help DBA teams master essential technical skills as well as crucial human skills such as. Training is tailored for your needs and tailored to boost day-to-day effectiveness. Contact us now to get started.

Do you provide onsite support?

We primarily offer remote training and consulting to help our clients save on costs. However, onsite support is available upon request if your project or team requires in-person assistance.

What services do you provide?

We provide a comprehensive suite of database services including DevOps DBA services, Cloud DBA services, AI DB services, SecOps DBA services, Managed DB services (MSP), and On-Demand DBA services tailored to meet your business needs.

How does remote support work?

All support is delivered securely over encrypted channels using VPN or SSH, based on your organization's setup. We integrate seamlessly with your team’s workflow and provide detailed updates, ensuring transparency and accountability throughout.

What industries do you work with?

We support clients across various industries including healthcare, finance, retail, and education. If your business relies on data, we can help you modernize and transform your database systems to align with your business goals.

Do you offer one-time projects or only ongoing support?

Yes, we offer both! Whether you need one-time help with a specific database project or ongoing DBA support to maintain and optimize your database environment, we'll provide flexible solutions tailored to your business needs.

How do I get started?

Just schedule a call or contact us to discuss your needs. We'll provide a no-obligation consultation to understand your goals and propose the right solutions.

How did RKKoranteng start?

RKKoranteng began when founder Richard Koranteng recognized that growing organizations were often overlooked when it came to accessing high-quality affordable database support. Seeing this gap, he set out to create a service that delivers the same level of expert solutions typically reserved for large enterprises - tailored specifically to the unique needs and budgets of smaller organizations.
